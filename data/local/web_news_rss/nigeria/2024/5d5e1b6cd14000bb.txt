Mike Pearson reports on groundbreaking University of Illinois research that identifies Africa as potentially the most significant long-term market opportunity for American agriculture. Africa's rapidly growing population and rising incomes are creating ideal conditions for American agricultural exports. For commodity producers seeking the next China-sized market, Africa represents enormous untapped potential. The University of Illinois analysis delivers a stark warning: the window for building meaningful U.S. agricultural relationships in Africa won't remain open indefinitely.
