NSIA, Asset Green Ltd seal deal on developing dairy production platform - APAnews - Agence de Presse Africaine #### NSIA, Asset Green Ltd seal deal on developing dairy production platform. # NSIA, Asset Green Ltd seal deal on developing dairy production platform. Facebook  Twitter  Email  LinkedIn  Telegram  WhatsApp. The Nigeria Sovereign Investment Authority (NSIA) has signed a Memorandum of Understanding with UK-based Asset Green Ltd. to develop an integrated dairy production platform. The British Deputy High Commissioner to Nigeria, Jonny Baxter, said in a statement on Wednesday that the project would transform Nigeria’s dairy sector and strengthen national food security. According to him, the initiative is among Nigeria’s most ambitious dairy investments, combining 20,000 hectares of climate-smart forage production with a 10,000-milking cow operation. Baxter described the NSIA-Asset Green collaboration as a “full-circle moment” in bilateral relations and a sign of shared commitment to sustainable growth. In his remarks, the Asset Green Director, Rod Bassett, said that the partnership would unlock Nigeria’s agricultural potential. Bassett, who is also the Chief Executive Officer of Agrium Capital Ltd., said that the Greenfield investment would develop infrastructure, boost local production and cut import dependence. https://apanews.net/nsia-asset-green-ltd-seal-deal-on-developing-dairy-production-platform/
