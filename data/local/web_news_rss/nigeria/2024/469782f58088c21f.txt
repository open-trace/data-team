According to a community announcement, the market will be at Hub City Brewery, 25 W. Church St., in downtown Hagerstown. The market aims to bring together fresh food, local makers and the community. The market is seeking a variety of vendors, including:. * Farmers offering fresh produce and agricultural products. * Farmer representatives reselling products purchased directly from farmers. * Vendors with flowers and plants. ## Hagerstown's farmers market application deadline is May 8 for opening day.
