In a March 19 letter led by the American Farm Bureau Federation and signed by 54 agriculture organizations, industry leaders outlined a growing list of challenges — from extreme weather to rising input costs and geopolitical disruptions — that they say are threatening the viability of U.S. farms. “This combination of sustained market pressures, weather-related challenges, and geopolitical uncertainty poses a serious threat to the upcoming growing season and the long-term viability of U.S. agriculture,” the letter states. “Food security is national security, and America’s farmers call on you to support additional resources during this turbulent time to ensure a strong, reliable and affordable domestic food supply for communities across the country.”. Without timely assistance, continued losses risk accelerating farm closures, reducing domestic production capacity and weakening the ability of farmers and ranchers across this great nation to provide food, clothes and fuel for the American people.”. https://www.agdaily.com/news/farm-groups-warn-u-s-agriculture-nearing-brink/
