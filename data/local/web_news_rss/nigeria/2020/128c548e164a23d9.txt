The expansion projects include setting up of a 700-tonne-per-day Phosphoric Acid Plant and a 2,200-tonne-per-day Sulphuric Acid Plant at the Cochin Division, a 1500 MTPD Urea Plant at Udyogamandal, and a 20,000-tonne Ammonia Storage Tank at Willingdon Island.
