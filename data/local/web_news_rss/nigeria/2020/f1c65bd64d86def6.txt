MANILA, Philippines — The Department of Agriculture (DA) is seeking to secure at least P1 billion of investments from the private sector in its upcoming national investment forum, where it will showcase some of its key commodity priorities. Agriculture Secretary Francisco Tiu Laurel Jr. said they plan to entice private sector investments into the sectors of cacao, mango, seaweed and coffee through the DA’s first-ever national investment forum. “We want to grow these four industries as soon as possible so they need investments both coming from local and foreign,” Tiu Laurel said on the sidelines of the launching of the national investment forum yesterday at the Asian Development Bank.
