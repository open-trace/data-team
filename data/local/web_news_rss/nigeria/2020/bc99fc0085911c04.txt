​Agriculture ministry instructs provincial departments to monitor fuel prices​ - phnompenhpost.com ## Agriculture ministry instructs provincial departments to monitor fuel prices. Fuel prices from March 14 to 16, 2026, at a Total gas station in Phnom Penh. The Ministry of Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries has instructed all capital and provincial departments of agriculture to closely observe the supply and pricing of fuel and gas in their respective jurisdictions, ensuring that prices align with those announced by the Ministry of Commerce. A March 14 instruction issued by agriculture minister Dith Tina explained ministry had held discussions with the commerce ministry and the Ministry of Mines and Energy regarding the concerns of farmers and agricultural communities. He instructed all agricultural departments to facilitate swift solutions if farmers or agricultural communities face challenges and difficulties in accessing fuel, by notifying the respective capital or provincial departments of mines and energy or commerce. * Agriculture ministry instructs provincial departments to monitor fuel prices. https://www.phnompenhpost.com/national/agriculture-ministry-instructs-provincial-departments-to-monitor-fuel-prices
