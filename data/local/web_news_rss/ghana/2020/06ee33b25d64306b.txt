Sustainability is not an overriding priority when it comes to bakers’ capital investment decisions, according to *Baking & Snack*’s 2026 Capital Spending Study, sponsored by BEMA and conducted by Cypress Research. Only 22% of bakers reported that sustainability-driven equipment/technology investments were important goals for their capital investment in 2026. Ultimately, bakers are making these investments with the intention of improved efficiency, even when decisions are sustainability-driven. “The most important driver is the equipment’s efficiency, and the sustainability aspect comes next.”. As Hellmer referenced, 52% of respondents reported that sustainability is not an important investment goal. When making sustainability-driven equipment/technology investments, 35% of respondents said efficiency was the motivation for the investment while 23% reported it was the functionality.
