Agriculture Ministry Offers 240,000 Won Eco-Friendly Support to 160,000 Pregnant Women - 조선일보 # Agriculture Ministry Offers 240,000 Won Eco-Friendly Support to 160,000 Pregnant Women. The Ministry of Agriculture, Food and Rural Affairs announced on the 18th that starting from the second half of this year, it will provide 240,000 Korean won in online mall points to 160,000 pregnant women nationwide to purchase eco-friendly agricultural products. Each pregnant woman will receive 240,000 Korean won worth of online mall points. The payment method allows 80% of the package order amount to be paid with points, while the remaining 20% (48,000 Korean won) must be covered by the individual. The packages include organic and pesticide-free agricultural products, organic processed foods, and organic or antibiotic-free livestock products. Pregnant women can either select individual items or choose a pre-assembled package. The Ministry of Agriculture, Food and Rural Affairs has been preparing for the project since December of last year, forming a task force involving 17 cities and provinces and eco-friendly agricultural organizations. https://www.chosun.com/english/market-money-en/2026/03/18/IA4KLQQN7FBIRNAAB2P7ZQCQUM/
